Analysis: Latitude vs. Humidity

Relationship of Latitude to Global Humidity on March 28, 2020

In general, there does not seem to be a linear correlation between latitude and humidity. However, there does appear to be wider variation of humidity north of the equator, perhapse indicating that humidity varies more during winter months. In the southern hemisphere, humidity generally held above 60%. Humidity levels may be more dependant on other factors (i.e. proximity to the ocean, altitude of the location, etc.)
