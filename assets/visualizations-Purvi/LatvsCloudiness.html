Analysis: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Relationship of Latitude to Global Cloud Levels on March 28, 2020

In general, there does not seem to be a direct linear correlation between latitude and cloudiness. Across all altitudes, there does seem to be a cluster of "cloudiness" measurements at 20%, 40%, 78% (high-70s) and 90%. However it is not clear as to whether this reflects a convergence of the conditions under which cloud layers form, or if it is simply a reflection of the method in which the "cloudiness" variable is measured. The formation of clouds is probably more directly dependant on other factors (i.e. terrain conditions, altitude, surface temperature, weather fronts, etc.)
