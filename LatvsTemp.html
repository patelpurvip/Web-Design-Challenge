Analysis: Latitude vs. Temperature

Relationship of Latitude to Global Temperature on March 28, 2020

Temperatures were generally the highest between -30 and 30 degerees latitude, corresponding with tropical zones, which tend to have higher temperatures year-round. Of the temperatures at the extreme regions of the globe (below -30 or above 30 degrees latitude), temperatures in the southern hempshere are much higher, reflecting the fact that it was still the summer in the global south on this date.
