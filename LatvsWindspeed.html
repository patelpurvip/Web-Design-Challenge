Analysis: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Relationship of Latitude to Global Wind Speeds on March 28, 2020

In general, there does not seem to be a direct linear correlation between latitude and wind speed. With a few outliers, wind speed generally did not seem to rise above 20-25mph. Wind speed is probably more directly dependant on other factors (i.e. terrain topography, air pressure, surface temperature, weather fronts, etc.)
